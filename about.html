WHO IS SAMIEJAA?

A little something about me :3

18-year-old musician & creator from Denmark (He/They)

Samiejaa has been drawn to music for as long as he can remember — but it wasn't until 2023 that he started turning that passion into something real. Since then, he's been in constant motion: experimenting, releasing, and refining his sound with every track he puts out.

His music lives somewhere between worlds — pulling from electronic, dance, and scene-influenced production while weaving in elements of indie, pop, and rock. He's not chasing a single genre. He's chasing a feeling, and the sound keeps evolving.

Outside of music, Samiejaa is studying communications, IT and design — learning the full process behind how things are built and experienced, from concept to finished product. He builds his own websites, designs his own visuals, and shapes his own creative identity from the ground up.

Everything you see here? That's him.
