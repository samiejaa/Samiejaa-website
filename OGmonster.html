Monster OG

★★★★☆

This is the original Monster Energy Drink, one of my go-to's. Has that recognizable "energy drink taste", but with a nice twist. It's more sweet than competitors versions of the taste, making it unique. I give this a 4/5 because you cant go wrong with it. Its perfect for a warm summerday as well as an ice cold winter. It is however a bit boring and not that different from other brands which is why its not a 5.
