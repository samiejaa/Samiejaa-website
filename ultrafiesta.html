Monster Ultra Fiesta Mango Flavour

★★★☆☆

The Monster Ultra Fiesta Mango Flavour tastes a lot like the Mango Loco so if you need a comparison thats the closest one. They are really similar in taste, the difference being mostly the way it feels in the mouth. It has a nice refreshing mango taste. The mango loco has a thicker feel and is in general more creamy where as this one is thinner and has a more watery feel in the best way possible. Personally it hink this is a hidden gem. Theres not enough talk about it at all. With all that said i give it a 3/5 since it is really solid but no where near my favorites.
